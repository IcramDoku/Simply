Hello my name is Icram Doku. I am the creator and author of this website. I found myself struggling with money and did some reseach. I found this 60/30/10 Rule and started following it. It worked greatly for me so I though I should share it.


As a high school student and college student (with a job) I still struggle to save enough money. I hate asking my parents for money at the end of the month. So, with some reseach I found this simple rule and found it very easy to follow!

Of course, this rule applies when you are a beginner (like me) to saving. If you are saving for a big purcase like a new car or a house, this will only help you to get in the habit of saving. You will gradualy have to increase the percent of your saving!
