A little bit about the 60/30/10 Rule. This rule will help you with saving and spending your money effecently. The percent division is 60% for essentials, 10% for fun, 30% to your saving.

Needs and what they include: rent, utilities, phone, internet, insurance, car payment, etc... If your essentials passes the 60% of your monthly income. Do not worry, it's time to improvise and move into a more affordable housing or take on a roommate. Negotiate price or take advantage of offers on your internet, phone, insurance, and more.

Wants and what they include: vacations, eating out, subscriptions, etc... If your wants are out of your buget, cut back. Eat out less, share some subscriptions like Netflix with a friend. This is the easiest fincially but hard emotionaly to cut back on, but we need to stay in budget.

Saving needs to be 30% of your income. But as you get older and your income increase invest a lot more into savings. Your older self will thank you!
